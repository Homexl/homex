Our Services

Automated Housing Society Accounting

We replace manual registers and scattered spreadsheets with automated, system-driven accounting for housing societies and apartment complexes.

  • Automated maintenance bill generation with correct interest, waivers, and arrears.
  • Online payment tracking and auto-allocation of receipts against member flats.
  • Automated ledgers, expense tracking, and bank reconciliation to reduce manual errors.
  • Ready financial statements: income–expenditure, balance sheet, trial balance, and unit-wise statements.
  • GST and audit-ready reports for society auditors and committee reviews.
